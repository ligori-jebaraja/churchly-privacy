1. Overview

Churchly is committed to creating a safe digital environment that protects children from sexual abuse and exploitation. This policy outlines our comprehensive approach to preventing, detecting, and responding to Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) within our church management platform.

2. Scope

This policy applies to:

  • All users of the Churchly application
  • Church administrators and staff members
  • Content shared within the platform
  • Communication features and interactions
  • Data handling and storage practices

3. Our Commitment

Zero Tolerance Policy

Churchly maintains a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation. We are committed to:

  1. Prevention: Implementing robust safeguards to prevent CSAE
  2. Detection: Using advanced monitoring and reporting systems
  3. Response: Taking immediate action when violations are identified
  4. Compliance: Meeting all legal and regulatory requirements

4. Prohibited Content and Activities

‚ö†Ô∏è Strictly Prohibited: The following activities are strictly prohibited on our platform and will result in immediate account termination and reporting to authorities.

Content Violations

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) in any form
  • Sexualized content involving minors
  • Content that exploits or endangers children
  • Grooming behaviors or communications
  • Inappropriate images or videos of minors

Behavioral Violations

  • Predatory behavior toward children
  • Solicitation of personal information from minors
  • Attempts to establish inappropriate contact with children
  • Sharing or distribution of CSAM
  • Facilitating child exploitation in any form

5. Safety Measures and Controls

Technical Safeguards

Content Monitoring

  • Automated scanning of uploaded images and files
  • AI-powered content detection systems
  • Regular audits of user-generated content
  • Secure image storage with restricted access

Access Controls

  • Role-based permissions system
  • Church administrator oversight requirements
  • Age verification for sensitive features
  • Encrypted data transmission and storage

Communication Safeguards

  • Monitored messaging systems
  • Restricted direct communication with minors
  • Transparent communication logs
  • Parental consent requirements for minor participation

Administrative Controls

User Verification

  • Identity verification for church staff
  • Background check requirements for administrators
  • Regular review of user permissions
  • Suspicious activity monitoring

Training and Awareness

  • CSAE awareness training for church administrators
  • Regular policy updates and communication
  • Best practices documentation
  • Incident response training

7. Response Procedures

Immediate Actions

Upon receiving a CSAE report, we will:

  1. Secure Evidence: Preserve all relevant data and communications
  2. Suspend Access: Immediately suspend suspected accounts
  3. Report to Authorities: Notify appropriate law enforcement within 24 hours
  4. Internal Investigation: Conduct thorough internal review
  5. User Protection: Implement measures to protect potential victims

Investigation Process

  • Dedicated CSAE response team
  • Coordination with law enforcement
  • Preservation of digital evidence
  • Regular case status updates
  • Comprehensive documentation

Follow-up Actions

  • Account termination for confirmed violations
  • Legal action when appropriate
  • Policy updates based on lessons learned
  • Victim support resources

8. Legal Compliance

Regulatory Adherence

We comply with all applicable laws and regulations, including:

  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Local child protection laws
  • Platform-specific policies (Google Play, App Store)

Law Enforcement Cooperation

  • Full cooperation with investigations
  • Timely response to legal requests
  • Preservation of evidence as required
  • Regular communication with authorities

9. Data Protection for Minors

Collection Limitations

  • Minimal data collection from users under 18
  • Parental consent requirements
  • Clear purpose statements for data use
  • Regular data retention reviews

Storage and Access

  • Encrypted storage of minor's data
  • Restricted access protocols
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure deletion procedures
